About Us

Who we are and what we do.

Our Mission

We strive to make sustainable living accessible to everyone through community engagement, eco-friendly initiatives, and local farming support.

Our Vision

Creating a greener future by connecting communities with sustainable practices, clean energy awareness, and local food sources.

Our Values

Community, sustainability, transparency, innovation, and fun guide everything we do. Yes, even fun matters!

Our History

A journey toward sustainable living.

Founded in 2018

Urban Harvest started as a small community garden in Colombo, with just 10 volunteers and a few seeds. The dream? Making eco-friendly living practical and fun.

Growth & Expansion

From a single garden, we now collaborate with over 50 local farms and community groups, holding workshops, cleanups, and seasonal events.
